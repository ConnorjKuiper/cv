Connor Jimmy Kuiper III of Connortopia

The Third of his Name, the Ultimate Conquerer of the Wildlands, the Lord Protector of the Empire of Connor

Connor Jimmy Kuiper III is a celebrated hero of the renowned Empire of Connor. He is known for conquering the frostbitten lands of the Knuckens, bringing peace to a region that had been ravaged by war for decades.

Education

Connor Jimmy Kuiper III has been well educated in all areas of the learned arts. He is a master alchemist, and attended many illustrious and respected Universities, including the Connor University of Connortopia. He is most well known for his ability to guess the approximate weight of horses he passes on the road with no help at all (definitely not), which has earned him much praise from his grateful subjects.
